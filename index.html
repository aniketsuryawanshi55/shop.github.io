About Us

Aniket Laundry, established in 1995, has been dedicated to offering reliable and high-quality laundry services for nearly three decades. Since our founding, we have strived to make laundry day easier and more convenient for our customers with advanced techniques, a customer-first approach, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Whether you need wash-and-fold services, dry cleaning, or ironing, Aniket Laundry ensures your clothes receive the care they deserve. We also provide hassle-free pick-up and delivery options for added convenience. Our goal is simple: to provide the best laundry solutions while maintaining a focus on quality, trust, and customer satisfaction.
